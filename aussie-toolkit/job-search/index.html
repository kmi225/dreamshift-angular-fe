Job Search Action Plan

We’ve created a simple Job Search Strategy for you, focusing on three key areas to help you land your next opportunity:
  1. Online Job Applications
  2. Utilizing Your Connections
  3. Reaching Out to Recruiters
This guide will walk you through everything you need to do each week as you work toward landing your next job. From job applications to follow-ups and networking — we’ve broken it down into clear, easy steps.

Weekly Overview

Before getting started, make sure keep track of your weekly goals:
  • Apply for highly relevant jobs
  • Follow up on previous applications
  • Reach out to recruiters and contacts
  • Track everything in a simple spreadsheet
Focus on quality applications over quantity. One strong, tailored application is better than ten generic ones.

Online Job Applications

Steps to follow

Search through job portals like Indeed, Seek, and LinkedIn Jobs.

Read each job description carefully

Tailor your docs to match the keywords

Keep a record of each job you applied

Email Templates

To: hiringmanager@company.com
Subject: Application for [Job Title] – Referral from [Referrer’s Name]

Dear [Hiring Manager’s Name],

I’m writing to express my interest in the [Job Title] role at [Company Name]. I have a strong background in [Brief Skills/Field], and I was encouraged to apply by [Referrer’s Name or Title], who spoke highly of your team.

I would appreciate the opportunity to discuss how my experience aligns with your team’s needs.

Kind regards,
[Your Name]

Utilizing Your Connections

Steps to follow

Make a list of connections - this can be Friends/Family in relevant industries, Alumni or university friends or LinkedIn or Instagram connections

Message them asking for insights or referrals

Ensure that it is short, friendly, and professional

Follow up in seven days if no response

Message Templates

Hi [Name],

I came across your profile while exploring opportunities in [Field] and saw you’re at [Company Name].

With [X]+ years of experience in [Relevant Areas], I’d love to hear what your experience has been like and any insights you have into the company.

Looking forward to connecting!

Best,
[Your Full Name]

Reaching Out to Recruiters

Steps to follow

Search on LinkedIn by title: "Recruiter", "Talent Acquisition", or through Company websites(look at the Careers or Team page).

Connect with recruiters on LinkedIn

Message them once connected

Save their emails if listed and follow up

Always be polite and specific

Email Templates

To: recruiter@company.com
Subject: Open to [Field] Roles | [X] Years Experience

Dear [Recruiter’s Name],

I came across your profile and noticed your experience recruiting for roles in [Region]. I’m currently exploring opportunities in [Field] and would love to connect.

I have [X] years of experience managing [Key Tasks] across [Countries/Industries].

Here’s my LinkedIn: [URL]

Looking forward to connecting!

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
